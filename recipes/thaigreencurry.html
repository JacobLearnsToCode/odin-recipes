Description

Thai Green Curry in 30 minutes made by freshening up store bought curry paste OR with a homemade green curry paste! Whichever way you go, the one essential step to make a really great green curry is to fry off the curry paste.

Make this with chicken or even entirely meat free. With a sauce this good, you can put anything in it â€“ and it will taste amazing!

Ingredients

  • Curry Sauce
  • White Rice
  • Tofu
  • Green Curry Paste
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Prepare and wash your ingredients
  2. Put all ingredients in a pan and heat
  3. Make sure everything is nice and cooked
  4. Serve it all up
